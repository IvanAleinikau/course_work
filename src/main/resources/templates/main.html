Welcome to site!

Each subsequent generation of the S-Class is a demonstration of the best achievements of the global automotive industry. This has been the case throughout the history of the model: from the late 1970s to the present day, ABS, ESP, airbags, rain and light sensors, keyless entry, seat ventilation and much more have debuted on the ESCs at various times. All this, years later, ended up with competitors, and then came to the mass market. On average, automakers have mastered the technology for five years, but it happened that much longer. The new Mercedes S-Class W223 is no exception in terms of technology: digital headlights, augmented reality and frontal airbags for rear passengers debuted here. None of the competitors have all this today. Let's figure out how this works on the example of the S450, which costs almost 18 million rubles.
